How I approach engineering

Thoughtful structure without losing pragmatism.

Understand

Start with the problem and constraints.

Technology choices make more sense after understanding users, requirements, risks and context.

Structure

Make boundaries and responsibilities explicit.

Clear interfaces and data flows make systems easier to maintain, debug and evolve.

Observe

Make system behavior visible.

I debug from important boundaries, trace the data or request, and progressively narrow the issue.

Balance

Apply practices proportionally.

Testing, automation and process should solve real problems rather than become detached rituals.
